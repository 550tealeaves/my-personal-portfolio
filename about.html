About Nastasia

New to the world of data analysis & visualization and hoping to apply these skills to public health and research to

After hearing from a co-worker about the SheCodes workshop and despite no prior experience, I embarked upon my initial coding journey, completing all four workshops. To further increase my knowledge, I enrolled in a program for data analysis and visualization. The goal is to apply these skills to create digital solutions to improve public health literacy.
